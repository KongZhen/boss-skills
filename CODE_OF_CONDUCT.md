# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Boss Skills is a satirical project about workplace archetypes. The humor targets *management patterns*, never individuals or identity groups.

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive behaviors

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what's best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Contributing workplace humor that punches at patterns, not people

### Unacceptable behaviors

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Content that targets specific real individuals or companies
- Discriminatory "jokes" disguised as boss personas
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Content-Specific Rules

Because this project involves satirical workplace content, we have additional rules:

1. **No real people.** Boss personas must be fictional archetypes, never based on identifiable individuals
2. **No company naming.** Don't name real organizations in skill content
3. **No identity-based humor.** The boss's annoyingness comes from their *management style*, not who they are
4. **No genuine malice.** If your contribution makes someone feel genuinely attacked rather than recognized, it needs revision

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
